Rexburg Area

This website is focused on serving students in and around BYU-Idaho and tourists traveling to West Yellowstone. Its primary goal is to help these individuals discover various activities in the area, offering alternatives to heavily congested tourist destinations. While many students and locals are familiar with popular spots like Fact Cats, Walmart, and Broulim's in Rexburg, they often miss out on the pleasures hidden in small businesses. This site aims to promote these hidden gems in the area, whether it's the outdoor paintball arena or numerous thrift stores.

Indoor Activities

As users come to this page, they will be able to find a wider variety of activities that they can do inside a building and hide from weather conditions outside. These activities, can be from eating in different types of local restaurants or seeing the fossil museum on campus to going to the indoor axe throwing arena. This page, is meant to help visitors and BYU-I students or even long-term community members find something to do in their spare time and enjoy it with others.
