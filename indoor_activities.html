Heber Hatchets

The East Idaho Aquarium is a wonderful place to have fun with date or family with more than 10 different interactive exhibits. You can pet Stingrays, feed animals, and interact with every type of animal. They have birds, lizards, porcupines, and every type of fish you can even think of.

Fatcats bowling

Welcome to Fat Cats, your all-in-one destination for ultimate entertainment! Immerse yourself in the thrill of striking pins on our state-of-the-art bowling lanes, venture through the twists and turns of our captivating mini golf course, and experience the magic of the silver screen in our cutting-edge movie theater. At Fat Cats, we've seamlessly woven together the excitement of bowling, the whimsy of mini golf, and the enchantment of cinema, creating an unparalleled experience under one roof. Whether you're seeking family fun, a night out with friends, or a memorable celebration, Fat Cats is where unforgettable moments are made. Roll, swing, and be captivated join us at Fat Cats, where the fun never ends!
